About Us

Knights Elite is a summer mentorship program aimed at helping middle school students build a strong foundation in track and field to get them prepared for high school athletics.

Coaching

Sprints

At Knights Elite, our state-qualifying sprinters have mastered the art of sprinting and are eager to share their expertise. We offer structured workouts covering everything from top-end speed to block starts, ensuring you excel in every aspect of your race.

Jumps

At knights elite, we provide comprehensive training programs for long, triple, and high jump. From mastering approach techniques to perfecting takeoff and landing mechanics, our structured workouts ensure development, empowering you to soar to new heights and distances in your athletic journey.

Hurdles

Our Hurdle coaches specialize in 110 and 300 hurdles, emphasizing proper form to prevent injuries and maximize performance. With personalized training programs, we'll refine your technique and enhance your confidence on the track. From mastering stride patterns to improving rhythm, we'll prepare you to excel in hurdle racing.
